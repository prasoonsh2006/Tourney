Tournament Format

Souls of SoulCity isn’t just a race — it’s a battle of speed, skill, and survival. From auction to finals, every turn decides destiny.

  • 🎯
    Auction Draft: Each team builds a squad of 6 racers.
  • 🚦
    Phase 1 – Qualifiers: All racers run 3 races each. Top teams advance, bottom eliminated, middle teams move to Knockouts.
  • 🔥
    Phase 2 – Knockouts: Surviving teams cut to 4 racers. Each runs 3 races. Top teams move forward, lowest eliminated.
  • 🏁
    Grand Finals – Bracket Battles: Finalist teams face off in bracket-style team races until one Champion remains.

Rules & Regulations

Souls of SoulCity runs on fairness and skill. Every team and racer must follow these rules:

  • Squad Limit: Max 6 racers per team.
  • Recharge: Buy extra SVC; leftover refunded.
  • Roster Lock: Teams are locked after auction.
  • Dashcam POV: 60 FPS or lower.
  • Vehicles: Only approved classes/modifications.
  • Conduct: No exploits or unfair play.
  • Management: Officials can update rules anytime.

Prize Pool

Here, champions earn more than glory — they claim rewards forged by the streets.

The final SVC prize pool is calculated based on the league's financial activity:

  • Prize Pool Contribution:60% of all SVC bidded on racers during the Auction is allocated directly to the final Prize Pool.
  • Fund Boost: Any additional SVC recharge amounts are also fully added to the final Prize Pool, maximizing total winnings.
  • Racer Earnings: The remaining 40% of the bidded SVC is paid directly to the respective racer.
