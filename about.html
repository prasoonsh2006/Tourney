Rules & Regulations

Souls of SoulCity runs on fairness and skill. Every team and racer must follow these rules:

  • Squad Limit: Max 6 racers per team.
  • Recharge: Buy extra SVC; leftover refunded.
  • Roster Lock: Teams are locked after auction.
  • Dashcam POV: 60 FPS or lower.
  • Vehicles: Only approved classes/modifications.
  • Conduct: No exploits or unfair play.
  • Management: Officials can update rules anytime.
